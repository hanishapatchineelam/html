Maharashtra government formation LIVE: Speaker's post point of contention between NCP, Congress

Will Maharashtra finally get a new government today? The Shiv Sena, the Nationalist Congress Party (NCP) and the Congress have been working on a prospective coalition. Maharashtra is currently under President's Rule, which was imposed earlier this month after the two parties that won the state election together -- the Sena and the BJP -- disagreed on a key issue and so couldn't form a government together. Stay tuned to this live blog for the latest updates from Maharashtra.

Congress wants Prithviraj Chavan as Speaker, NCP disagrees

Congress wants Speaker position for Prithviraj Chavan and NCP doesn't want Chavan to be the Speaker, according to sources. On Saturday, Congress and NCP will hold a meeting. Later, Shiv Sena will be called for the meeting.
